How Tiers Are Set
Preferred — top-quartile network performance on 30-day readmission rate and cost/day, CMS star rating of 4+ where applicable, and sufficient capacity for priority referrals.
Standard — meets network minimums; eligible for referrals but not prioritized over Preferred-tier facilities.
Monitor — one or more metrics trending outside network thresholds; referrals continue with added care-navigator oversight.
Do Not Refer — below minimum performance thresholds; referrals paused pending a formal performance review.
